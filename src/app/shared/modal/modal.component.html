Rules

Objective

The objective of the game is to be the first player to reach the selected amount (e.g., 10 euros) through strategic betting.

Gameplay

  • Turns:
    • Players take turns to make their moves.
  • Placing Bets:
    • On their turn, each player can place a bet.
    • The bet must be within the following range:
      • Minimum bet: 0.10 euros.
      • Maximum bet: 15% of the selected amount (e.g., 1.5 euros for a 10 euro game).
  • Hidden Bets:
    • layers only know the bets they place.
    • Bets are kept hidden from the opponent.

Winning the game

  • The game continues until one player accumulates the selected amount (e.g., 10 euros) through their bets.
  • The first player to reach or exceed the selected amount wins the bank (the total of both players' starting amounts).
